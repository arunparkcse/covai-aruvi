About Us

A Tradition of Pure Flavours

Covai Aruvi is a trusted name in Coimbatore for premium-quality spices and dry fruits. We carefully source the finest ingredients and repack them in freshness-sealed pouches so every pinch and every bite delivers the authentic taste of nature.

From everyday cooking essentials like cumin and cinnamon to premium dry fruits like almonds and cashews, our entire range is priced affordably — starting at just ₹5 — because good food should be accessible to every home.
